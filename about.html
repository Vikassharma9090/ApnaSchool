About Our School

Welcome to GYAN SAROWAR PUBLIC SCHOOL

GYAN SAROWAR PUBLIC SCHOOL, Nabada,Muzzafurpur with an investment of millions of rupees with an eye on worthful return in terms of money. Our School provides English classes for fluent English speaking children between the ages of 3 to 11 being brought up in a bilingual environment. Many of the children have one or two English-speaking parents and English is spoken at home. Others have returned from abroad where schooling was in English and wish to maintain their fluency. The classes are small and children develop their speaking, reading and writing abilities in English. The teachers are all native English speakers. They are qualified with professional experience and are highly dedicated to creating a fun, friendly, but also hardworking environment at Our School. The standard is high and all children must have good spoken English. The work in primary classes is more formal with emphasis on grammar, spelling and comprehension, preparing for international classes at secondary school.
